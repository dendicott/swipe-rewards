Swipe Rewards is a program designed to help you maximize your credit card bonus earning by showing you your bonus points earning performance over the last 5 years, as well as let you know what annual fees you have coming up in the next year, and what major credit card provider you may be eligible for a new card approval to keep on earning. You can enter your credit card history into the GUI, or by closing out the GUI without saving the program will load a test file so you can see the output.
